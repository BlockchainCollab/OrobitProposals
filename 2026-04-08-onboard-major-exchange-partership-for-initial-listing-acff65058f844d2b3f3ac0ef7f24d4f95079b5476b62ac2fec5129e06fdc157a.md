# Background

As our network grows, so does the demand for wider liquidity. While DEX volume has shown strong steady organic growth, several Tier-1 Centralized Exchanges (CEX) have expressed interest in onboarding the Orobit Governance token.

# Polling Objectives

We are seeking a community consensus before entertaining any non-disclosure agreements or legal listing processes, as a CEX listing requires allocating 5% of the ecosystem's marketing reserve for market making and compliance integrations.

## Option: FOR

Approving this poll signals the core development foundation to engage in negotiations with the leading CEX candidates to list our token within the next operational quarter.

## Option: AGAINST

Rejecting this poll signals that the community prefers we remain dedicated entirely to our current decentralized exchange footprint and preserves the treasury supply originally earmarked for these CEX onboarding events.

### Next Steps

This proposal is strictly a sentiment check. It carries no executive payload or spell contract binding. Should the proposal pass with a supermajority (60%+), an official Executive Proposal will follow outling the exact selected CEX and token allocation logic for final voting.
